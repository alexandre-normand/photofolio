The Old Friend

After spending 5 weeks camping in this Vanagon, it does feel like an old friend. It's not without flaws and it did misbehave on a few occasions but we got to meet with friendly people along the way as a result. And really, having friends do crazy things once in a while are part of what makes life interesting, right?

The One With the Japanese Maple Tree

We have a friendly Japanese Maple Tree that is home in our yarn. It's a dwarf tree so it's small and has the shape of an umbrella. This tree makes the perfect small hiding place and/or shelter. As you can tell from the look here, adults might not always be welcome.
