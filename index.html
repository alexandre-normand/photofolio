Morning Sun

For many little reasons, I feel more joy taking pictures of smaller beautiful moments rather than majestic ones. Probably because the majestic scenes are so difficult to capture well in a single picture. It's much easier to reduce the scope of your ambitions and focus on something more manageable. But that's only one reason. There are a lot of ordinary scenes that go underappreciated because they happen everywhere or happen all the time.
